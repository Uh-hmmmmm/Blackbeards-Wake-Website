Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove ads?

Tap the gear icon in the top corner to open Settings, then tap "Remove Ads". It's a one-time purchase of $1.99 that permanently removes all advertisements.

I purchased Remove Ads but I'm still seeing ads.

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". This will verify your purchase with Apple and re-enable the ad-free experience. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

How do I play?

Use the on-screen joystick to steer your ship. Sail in a loop around enemies and objects to encircle and capture them. The bigger your loops, the more points you earn. Avoid getting hit by enemies — you only have 3 lives!

Is my game progress saved?

High scores are saved locally on your device. Your "Remove Ads" purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device using "Restore Purchases" in the Settings.

Does the app collect my personal data?

We do not collect personal information. The app uses Google AdMob for advertising, which may collect device identifiers for ad targeting. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
